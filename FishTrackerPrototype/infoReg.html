What Is Required To Fish

In Ontario it is pretty simple to get the nessesary equipment in order to fish. Firstly you will need a rod, reel, line, and bait. All of this can be purchased from any store that carries fishing equiptment, but to name a few there is Canadian Tire, Bass Pro Shops, and even Walmart has some of this equipment.
